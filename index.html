About Badolato Electrical Services

Badolato Electrical Services ("BES") is a licensed, bonded, and insured electrical contractor serving homeowners, seniors, commercial businesses, property managers, marine facilities, and industrial operations across Southwest Florida.

Founded by Electrician Michael Badolato, BES represents the newest chapter in a family legacy of entrepreneurship. Michael is a third-generation Venice and Sarasota area business owner, carrying forward a tradition of community commitment, integrity, and hands-on craftsmanship that has defined the Badolato name for decades. His path to the electrical trade began with a fascination with Nikola Tesla's groundbreaking work in alternating current and power transmission‚Äîa curiosity that evolved into apprenticeship, journeyman training, 8 years of hands on training and experience and the launching of BES to serve the community.

Today, Michael brings that same precision and respect for the craft to every project BES undertakes. Whether it's a ceiling fan installation for a senior citizen or a complete panel upgrade for a commercial property, every job receives the same level of attention, documentation, and code-compliant execution.
