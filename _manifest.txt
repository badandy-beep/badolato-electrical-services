FLORIDA LICENSE DISCLOSURE REQUIREMENTS (Reference)
================================================================================

Per Florida Statute §489.119:
- License number must appear on all advertising, bids, proposals, contracts
- Applies to all media including websites, social media, print
- Failure to comply can result in fines or license suspension

Per Florida Statute §489.127:
- Cannot falsely hold yourself out as licensed
- Cannot present another's certificate as your own
- CAN legally operate under a qualifying contractor's license

The disclosure language used on this site:

"All electrical work performed under Florida Certified Electrical 
Contractor License #EC-XXXXXX held by [QUALIFYING CONTRACTOR NAME]. 
Michael Badolato operates as a qualified electrician under this license 
pursuant to Florida Statute Chapter 489."

This language:
✓ Clearly identifies the actual license holder
✓ Shows the license number as required
✓ Explains Michael's role (qualified electrician)
✓ References the governing statute
✓ Is standard industry practice in Florida
